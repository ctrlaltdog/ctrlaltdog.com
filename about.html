about me:

hi there! you can call me bailey. i'm agender and use they/them pronouns.

i'm a queer trans furry anarchist living in Calgary AB, Canada.

i like photography, drawing/painting fluffy animals, writing fiction, tinkering with computers, fighting with linux, playing guitar, cycling, skateboarding, and unfortunately, gaming.
