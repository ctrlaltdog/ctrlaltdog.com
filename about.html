about me:

hi there! you can call me bailey. i'm agender and use they/them pronouns.

i'm a queer trans furry anarchist living in Calgary AB, Canada.

my interests include landscape, urban, and nature photography, drawing/painting fluffy animals, writing fiction, tinkering with computer hardware, fighting with linux, playing guitar, cycling, skateboarding, and unfortunately, gaming.
